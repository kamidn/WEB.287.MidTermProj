Special Meeting Notice

The Tyrrell County Board of Commissioners, upon call of the Chairman, shall convene for a special meeting on Monday, December 5, 2022 at 11:30 a.m. in the Commissioners Chambers of the County Administration Building located at 106 South Water Street, Columbia, NC. The meeting is called for the sole purpose of approving the Sheriff's Bond. Any questions should be directed to Penny Owens, Clerk to the Board, at 252-796-1371, ext. 2321.
